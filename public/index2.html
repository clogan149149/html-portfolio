Best Movies of All-Time According to Chris

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Warrior

This is one of my favorite movies because not only is it entertaining, it also hits personally close to home.

The Dark Knight

This movie was just flat-out amazing! From the acting, to the story, the Joker and Batman are portrayed in such an entertaining way.

Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

This movie has the perfect combination of action and comfort. I would not recommend many other movies as background noise.
